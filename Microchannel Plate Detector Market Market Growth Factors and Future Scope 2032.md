Market Restraints
Despite the growing demand, the Microchannel Plate Detector Market faces challenges, including:
High Production Costs – The complex manufacturing process of MCP detectors results in high costs, limiting widespread adoption.
Limited Availability of Raw Materials – The production of MCP detectors requires specialized glass and coatings, making supply chain disruptions a potential challenge.
Competition from Alternative Technologies – Emerging detection technologies such as silicon photomultipliers (SiPMs) may pose a threat to MCP adoption.

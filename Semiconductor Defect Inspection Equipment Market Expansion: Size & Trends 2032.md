Key Market Drivers

Challenges and Restraints
While the market is on a growth trajectory, it faces certain restraints:
High Cost of Advanced Inspection Equipment: The sophisticated nature of defect inspection tools makes them expensive, posing a challenge for smaller semiconductor manufacturers.
Technological Limitations in Detecting Nanometer-scale Defects: As chip sizes shrink, inspection tools must evolve to detect ultra-small defects effectively.
Supply Chain Disruptions: Global semiconductor supply chain constraints can impact the availability of inspection equipment and spare parts.

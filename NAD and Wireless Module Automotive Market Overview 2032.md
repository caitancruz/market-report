Rising Demand for Vehicle Telematics: Telematics systems depend on wireless modules for real-time communication and data transmission, making NADs indispensable in modern automotive design.

Major Restraints Hindering Market Expansion
Despite the robust outlook, the market faces a few key challenges:
High Integration Costs: The upfront cost of installing NADs and wireless modules, especially with 5G capabilities, may deter adoption in low-cost or emerging-market vehicles.
Data Security Concerns: As vehicles become more connected, they become vulnerable to cyber threats, posing risks to both drivers and manufacturers.
Limited Infrastructure in Developing Economies: Many regions still lack the 5G or V2X infrastructure required for advanced automotive connectivity.

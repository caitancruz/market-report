Market Restraints
Despite strong growth prospects, the disposable hand warmer market faces certain challenges:
Environmental Concerns – The single-use nature of disposable hand warmers contributes to waste generation, leading to sustainability concerns among eco-conscious consumers.
Fluctuating Raw Material Prices – Variations in the costs of materials like iron powder and activated carbon can affect production costs and pricing strategies.
Competition from Reusable Hand Warmers – The increasing adoption of rechargeable and reusable hand warmers may limit the market growth of disposable variants.

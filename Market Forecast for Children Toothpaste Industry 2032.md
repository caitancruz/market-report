Stringent Regulatory Standards: Approval processes for children's oral care products are becoming increasingly rigorous.
High Product Pricing: Premium children's toothpaste products may be cost-prohibitive in low-income regions.
Limited Awareness in Rural Areas: Many rural markets still lack basic oral health education and access to specialized products.
Manufacturers are focusing on product innovation and price optimization to overcome these hurdles.

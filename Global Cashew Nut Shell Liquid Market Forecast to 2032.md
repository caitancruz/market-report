Cashew Nut Shell Liquid (CNSL) is a renewable, versatile, and cost-effective bio-based material derived from the cashew industry’s waste stream. It is rich in phenolic compounds and finds applications in coatings, friction linings, resins, surfactants, and more.

Growing Focus on Sustainable Materials Driving Demand
With increasing global efforts toward reducing dependency on petrochemicals, CNSL is emerging as a key eco-friendly alternative in multiple industries. Its renewable origin, excellent chemical reactivity, and low toxicity profile make it ideal for green chemistry applications.
Industries such as automotive, paints and coatings, and industrial chemicals are increasingly integrating CNSL in their formulations to meet environmental compliance standards.
Rising Industrial Applications Boost Market Expansion
The friction material sector remains one of the largest consumers of CNSL due to its thermal stability and binding properties. Likewise, CNSL-based resins are gaining momentum in the construction and electronics sectors, where heat resistance and chemical durability are paramount.
Additionally, the agrochemical industry is exploring CNSL as a raw material for developing biodegradable pesticides and insect repellents.

Market Restraints
Despite strong growth prospects, the market faces several challenges:
Seasonal Demand Fluctuations – Sales of frozen novelties are highly seasonal, with peak demand in summer months and a slowdown during colder seasons.
Rising Raw Material Costs – Fluctuations in dairy, sugar, and packaging material prices impact overall production costs.
Stringent Regulations – Food safety and labeling requirements vary across regions, posing compliance challenges for manufacturers.
